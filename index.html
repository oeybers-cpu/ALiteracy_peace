What is Translanguaging?

Translanguaging is a theoretical framework that views multilingual speakers' language practices as a unified system rather than separate compartments. It challenges the traditional separation of languages and recognizes the fluidity of language use in multilingual contexts.

In academic literacy, translanguaging enables students to draw on their full linguistic repertoire to engage with complex academic concepts, fostering deeper understanding and critical thinking.
